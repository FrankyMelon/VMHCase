Rehab and Therapy

The Valley Memorial Hospital Rehabilitation Department provides a wide range of inpatient and outpatient services. Our fully licensed staff strives to help patients identify their rehab needs and help them reach their maximum level of function. We provide services in a pleasant, friendly environment that respects the needs of each patient as an individual. Valley Memorial Hospital offers Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, and Respiratory Therapy. Regain your active lifestyle and independence through our expert rehab programs.

Physical Therapy: Valley Memorial Hospital can treat a wide variety of problems ranging from non-surgical to surgical, including:

  • Joint replacements (hips, knees, and shoulders)
  • Neck and back injuries
  • Shoulder problems
  • Muscle sprains and strains
  • Gait and balance safety training to maintain independence
  • Neurological problems such as strokes and Parkinson’s disease
  • Sports injuries

Occupational Therapy: Therapists work with individuals who need specialized assistance to lead independent, productive, and satisfying lives. The focus is on restoring function in activities of daily life. Valley Memorial Hospital treats patients who have experienced the following:

  • Broken bones, sports injuries, falls, or accidents
  • Strokes and heart attacks
  • Arthritis, multiple sclerosis, or other serious illnesses
  • Amputations or spinal injuries
  • Work-related injuries
  • Dementia or Alzheimer’s

Valley Memorial Hospital also assists with one-on-one self-care needs to keep patients as functional as possible. We help people re-learn everyday tasks such as cooking, cleaning, and light housekeeping.

Speech Therapy: Speech therapy provides a wide range of treatment targeting speech, language, and swallowing problems, including:

  • Swallowing evaluation and treatment for individuals who choke, strangle, or cough when eating or drinking
  • Modified Barium Swallow Test: an x-ray that shows the complete swallowing process when given a variety of food and liquid consistencies
  • Augmentative communication-alternative means of communicating without the use of voice

Respiratory Therapy: This department provides invasive and non-invasive pulmonary disease treatments to patients of all ages, either inpatients or outpatients. The department offers respiratory care for the entire hospital, including the Emergency Room, ICU, Geriatric Psychiatry, Restorative Care, and the medial floors. Valley Memorial Hospital treats the following diseases and illnesses that compromise the breathing system:

  • Pulmonary Asthma
  • Bronchitis
  • Cancer
  • COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease)
  • Drowning
  • Smoke Inhalation
  • Trauma
  • Circulation Problems
  • Heart Functions and Rhythm

Such diseases are treated with the following laboratory and specialized testing:

  • Holter Monitor: a 24-hour recording device that measures heart rhythms
  • PFT: Pulmonary Function Test: to measure the capacities and strength of the lungs
  • Cardiac and Pulmonary Stress Testing: to measure the strength of the heart, determine susceptibility to heart-attack, and to diagnose exercise-induced breathing disorders
  • Nebulization Treatment: to treat pneumonia, asthma and bronchitis

Our teams of doctors, nurses, surgeons, and administrators work
around the clock to give you and your family the specialized care you need.
