Evert-Green Consulting’s internship program acts as an investment in the future. With the counseling and mentorship from seasoned professionals, students can learn from hands-on experience and add their own creativity, value, and innovation to the company, propelling it forward into the future of the industry. Interns get to work with the CEO directly, gaining the benefit of her experience and leadership.
CEO Daysha Evert stepped into leadership at Evert-Green Consulting after a long career in marketing and research. She has championed the use of digital technology and proprietary solutions to continuously innovate for the ever-changing industry landscape. Under her guidance, Evert-Green Consulting has assembled a team of subject matter experts and creators who provide the best service possible to their clients.
Evert-Green Consulting is known for its innovation, emphasis on diversity, and commitment to quality. For more than ten years, under the leadership of Daysha Evert, the company has made great strides to serve the needs of its clients. Whether the client is a nonprofit organization or a for-profit organization, Evert-Green Consulting makes sure that they develop and implement strategies and solutions that make sense.
