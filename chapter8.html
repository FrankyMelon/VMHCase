Chapter 8: Forecasting at Valley Memorial Hospital

Details of Your Next Assignment

After you’ve finished your report for Materials Management head Bhavna Kumari, CEO Daysha Evert forwards you an invite to another teleconference. “You’ll like this assignment,” she writes. “The hospital needs help with their air transportation operations.”

You click on the link to the invite to meet with the Director of Valley Memorial Helicopter Service, Jamal Hamilton. He quickly introduces himself and jumps right into the meeting.

Assignment Questions For Chapter 8

  1. Develop a forecast of the number of helicopter flights monthly for the next 12 months. Hamilton will supply you with data on past flights, as well as on missed flights.
  2. Present your results in a memo that explains the forecasting technique used, the basis for its selection, and illustrates the reliability of your forecast.
