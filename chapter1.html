Your internship with Evert-Green Consulting will begin on a project with one of the company’s major healthcare clients, Valley Memorial Hospital. Assignment Questions For Chapter 1

Review the information on Valley Memorial Hospital.

  1. Identify the type of organization VMH is and its chief characteristics. Where would VMH map in a construct such as Figure 1.3?
  2. What does this organization do, in a broad sense? Visualizing the hospital as a transformation process, draw a simple diagram (like Figure 1.2) showing the inputs, resources, and outputs. Explain how customer feedback and performance information might be collected.
  3. Where is Valley Memorial Hospital located? What implications might result from the organization's location? Identify VMH's customer base.
  4. Identify all the different departments at VMH. With so many departments, how do you expect the VMH facility to be laid out?
  5. Identify the organizational structure. What are the major units, what type of personnel work there, how many staff, etc?
