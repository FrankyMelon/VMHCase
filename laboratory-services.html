Laboratory Services

Valley Memorial Hospital is pleased to offer a full-service lab facility. Our staff provides a high-quality experience and same-day results for many tests. We rely on state-of-the-art automation and innovative equipment to provide you with the most accurate results possible so that you can start to receive the care and treatment you need.

The Valley Memorial Hospital Laboratory is a full-service facility that offers services for both inpatients and outpatients, including:

  • Transfusion Services: testing of patient samples for compatibility and managing adverse effects of transfusion.
  • Hematology: testing for anemia, low platelet counts, an indication of infection, and abnormal cell studies.
  • Coagulation: testing for levels of coumadin and heparin.
  • Serology Testing: Mono, HIV, strep, pregnancy, drug screens and COVID-19.
  • Chemistry: testing for the kidney, liver, and heart, as well as diabetic testing, glycohemoglobin, and microalbumin.
  • Therapeutic Drug Levels: including digoxin, Dilantin, and Phenobarbital.
  • PSA Testing: testing for prostate cancer.
  • Blood Gases: for amounts of oxygen in the blood, PH levels, and carbon dioxide.
  • Microbiology: identification of bacteria and sensitivities.
  • Quantitative HCG Testing: test to determine exact age of a fetus.
  • Thyroid Testing: test to check thyroid function.
  • Urinalysis: tests urine for a wide range of disorders like urinary tract infections.

Our teams of doctors, nurses, surgeons, and administrators work
around the clock to give you and your family the specialized care you need.
