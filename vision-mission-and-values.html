Vision, Mission and Values

Valley Memorial Hospital is the leading healthcare provider in the region and one of the top hospitals in the state. Our vision is to make a positive difference in the health and wellness of each individual we serve. Our team of 4,000 healthcare providers, staff, assistants, and part-time workers serve more than 3 million patients a year through our network of telemedicine providers, emergency room services, urgent care clinics, and primary and specialty care centers.

We have achieved this vision through these four steps:

Innovation
Putting Patients First
Striving for Excellence
Teamwork

Our Mission

Valley Memorial Hospital has made its mission caring for the community, no matter what their financial status might be. We provide world-class healthcare, wellness programming, and assistance to all members of the community, regardless of their ability to pay. Every year, Valley Memorial Hospital offers subsidized health care services, free charity care to those who qualify, clinical and telemedicine programs to residents, and investments in research and innovative forms of care.

Our mission further demands that we go above and beyond to help the community stay healthy.

We recognize that our patients are unique individuals with specific needs.

We constantly improve the delivery of our inpatient, outpatient, and community care.

We build partnerships with others in the community and encourage participation.

Our Values

Valley Memorial Hospital openly denounces racism and discrimination in all forms. We recognize that these evils prevent our society from living healthy lives. That’s why our team has made a commitment to developing respect and compassion for those we serve, integrity and honesty in everything we do, and pride and fairness in our approach.

Our teams of doctors, nurses, surgeons, and administrators work
around the clock to give you and your family the specialized care you need.
