Nursing Services

Valley Memorial Hospital's nursing staff is a group of dedicated individuals committed to going the extra mile for their patients. Our patients can always expect friendly and caring nurses to address individual needs and concerns. As a group of highly regarded professionals, the nurses of Valley Memorial pride themselves on excellent service.

Valley Memorial Hospital boasts a talented, diverse, and professional nursing community. Our nurses are aligned with our mission and seek to carry our values throughout their work. Professional development and advancement are encouraged among our nurses as part of a way to continuously innovate and invest in the community’s wellbeing.

Valley Memorial offers a wide range of services and care for the surrounding community. Nurse care is provided for the following:

  • Surgery-inpatient and outpatient for general, orthopedic, ophthalmology, and urology
  • Intensive Care Unit—Stepdown
  • Restorative Care-Provide a nursing home level of care for patients on an interim basis until they are ready to go home OR TO AN assited living, or nursing home
  • Psychiatric Unit—for geriatric patients
  • Emergency Room—for prompt service and timely intervention of life-saving techniques when necessary. The emergency nurses work hand-in-hand with the Salinas County EMS and assist with the helipad when patients are flown in or out of Valley Memorial Hospital

Our professional practice model relies on shared leadership, meaningful recognition and compensation for our nurses, collaborative relationships, effective communication, a culture of safety and excellence, and an efficient team approach. We also offer the following Nursing Support Services:

  • Employee Health
  • House Supervisors
  • Nurse Educator
  • Infection Control
  • Case Management
  • Medicare Data Set Coordinator
  • Nurse Managers for each Nursing unit
  • Special Projects and Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations (JCAHO) Coordinator

Our teams of doctors, nurses, surgeons, and administrators work
around the clock to give you and your family the specialized care you need.
